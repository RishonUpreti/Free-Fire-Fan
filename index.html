About Free Fire

Free Fire is a popular battle royale mobile game developed by 111 Dots Studio and published by Garena. Players are dropped on an island and must fight to be the last one standing. With fast-paced gameplay, diverse characters, and a variety of weapons and strategies, Free Fire keeps millions of players worldwide hooked.
